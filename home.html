Welcome to CareerForge

The proposed system is an innovative, web-based College Placement Cell Management System. This system is specifically designed to streamline and enhance the placement process in engineering colleges, making it more efficient and effective.

Automatic Notification

The students who are selected for an interview receive a notification in the form of an email or text message automatically. This ensures timely communication and allows students to prepare well for their interviews.

Interactive Interface

The system provides an interactive interface for students, allowing them to update their profile, post their achievements, and showcase their skills and bio. This feature enables students to maintain an up-to-date profile that can be easily accessed by recruiting companies.

Mock Interview Preparation

The platform offers various quizzes, aptitude tests, and mock interview Q/A to help students prepare better for their interviews. It provides mock tests based on the company's nature, designed to increase students

CV Generation

The system includes a built-in CV generation functionality. Students can choose from predefined CV templates available within the system, which automatically populate with their profile information. This empowers students to efficiently create professional CVs, enhancing their preparation for job interviews.
